Background

I was brought on as a contractor to help Lead Product Designer, James Carthew, flesh out Envirosuite's ES5 Design system in Figma. This data heavy product also posed some interaction design challenges for us to solve and explore through various interactive prototypes.

About Envirosuite

Envirosuite is a platform that allows users to monitor various environmental pollution levels such as noise, odour and air pollution amongst others. It is a B2B product predominantly used by industry experts to guage the environmental impact their business operations are having.
