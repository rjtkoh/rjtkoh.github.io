UI Motion

A collection of UI animations designed to inform and instruct

Platforms

Windows, web

Responsibilities

Motion design, animation, illustration.

Period

2022-2023

Background

The following are motion design pieces used by body scanning start-up, Bodd. Bodd have a bespoke piece of hardware which scans bodies for measurments and wellness insights. At times throughout the user journey, animations have been used to guide and instruct users on how to pose and what to expect.

This branding element was animated to inform the user that something is happening while their body is being scanned.

A Google inspired uploading animation to occupy the user's attention while they wait for their scan to be uploaded.

A piece showing the various insights that can be derived from a wellness scan
